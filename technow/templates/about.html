Who We Are

We are a collective of global AI enthusiasts committed to leveraging the existing AI systems to help community based organizations address their challenges. By doing so, we can contribute to bridge parities that currently exist between AI and communities around the globe in terms of knowledge, access, and usage. Whether you come from a background in computer science, mathematics, psychology, linguistics, chemistry,philosophy, or you have curiosity, there are many ways to contribute to the advancement of AI.

Why DatAI Equity Alliance(D.E.A)

Governments worldwide are crafting thorough national strategies aimed at fostering inclusive, responsible and equitable AI development. Meanwhile, Silicon Valley continues to unveil AI-powered tools, and businesses are swiftly integrating AI to their operations. However, there's a notable disparity between the rapid advancement of AI systems and the slower pace at which ordinary citizens are benefiting from this exciting new tech era. Consequently, there's a pressing need to engage with the public about the benefits of AI on a personal or through organizations that serve them directly.
